info Information

Checkmarks is an overhaul kit for your imported, synced or invalid bookmarks. It batch-loads your favorites into a configurable number of tabs and triggers the native mechanism of loading favicons.

A list of request errors offers the opportunity to manually review the corresponding bookmarks and to update or remove them if they are broken.

WebExtensions cannot delete invalid favicons. You have to manually delete favicons.sqlite in your profile folder first.

Be aware that continuously loading tabs in parallel consumes a considerable amount of resources!
