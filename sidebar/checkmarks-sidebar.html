Hints

  • Symbols for errors are explained in a tooltip when you hover the icon.
  • Adjust the width of your sidebar to better see longer bookmark-titles and to avoid the disappearance of icons.
  • You can press ctrl+j to see more details about your bookmarks in the logs. If you enter "checkmarks" in the search-input, only logs of this extension are displayed. Adjust the log-level to your needs.
