Warning

Clicking the trash icon permanently removes the corresponding entry from your bookmarks.

After the confirmation of the first removal this warning will not appear again.

Symbols

play_circle_outline Starts loading bookmarks and favicons.
highlight_off Cancels loading bookmarks and favicons (not paused).
help_outline Shows this help dialog.
settings Shows the options page.
delete Deletes bookmarks permanently (after confirmation).
check_circle Removes entry from list (checked).
launch Launches bookmark in a new tab.
folder_open Hover to display the directory-path!
save Updates the bookmark with the url of the tab you've previously opened with the launch-button (appears after launch, see above).


Hints

  • Orange error-symbols are explained in a tooltip when you hover the icon.
  • Adjust the width of your sidebar to better see longer bookmark-titles. The list of errors can be scrolled horizontally.
  • You can press ctrl+j to see more details about errors in the browser console. If you enter "checkmarks" in the search-input, only logs of this extension are displayed. Adjust the log-level according to your needs. With content-blockers you will see many aborted sub-requests on the info-level.
